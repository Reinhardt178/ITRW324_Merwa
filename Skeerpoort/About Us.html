Vision & Mission

Our vision is to strive to be a trusted, reliable and committed supplier of bulk and aviation fuel and packed lubricants within Southern Africa. We place the customer first. Our mission is to be an ethical and responsive moral contributor to commercial & industrial business growth, and the continued contributor to overall economic growth in South Africa. Skeerpoort upholds the philosophy and principle of BEE and regards this as an opportunity and not a condition for success.

ETHICS & PHILOSOPHY

The company subscribes to the highest ethical standards, will display integrity, and will be honest and principled in all its dealings with staff, customers, suppliers, and all other stakeholders. The company supports excellence in its procurement, management and sales operations through staff training and adherence to modern methodologies. The company believes in full social responsibility and the upliftment, support of the local community and the training and empowerment of staff.
